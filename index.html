"Ad Astra Per Aspera" is latin for "Through hardships to the stars".

Building new friendships

Here at Doctor Challoner's we have a variety of different activities which help bond new relationships.

Learning

Doctor Challoner's not only provides a good education in different departments but also gives those attending an enjoyable and safe place to learn. Whether it be through the teaching or the activities that are given to challenge your son.

Sport

Challoner's provides many sporting opportunities, whether it be the chance to play football in Holland or play cricket in the Caribbean.

What could you do at DCGS?

Sports
Sports Clubs

There are many sports opportunities for your child to enjoy. Some of the clubs that your child may be interested in could be: Cricket, Tennis, Rugby and many more. Joining a school team could be an inspiration to your child and a great opportunity too. Your child could be given the chance to play abroad! Or more locally in places such as Twickenham.

Extra-Curricular
Extra-Curricular

Whether it be joining Chess, debating or Kendo club your son will thouroughly all. Some clubs are run by Sixth Formers who are dedicated in wanting your child to have fun and others are run by Teachers of the school. Such clubs can be accessed at the lunch break or after school.

Enrichment
Enrichment

Enrichment is offered to years 7,8 and 10. Your son will be given a verity of activities they can do for one day. Some activities in the past have been a trip to France for the day and others have been a trip to Twickenham. All these trips have been designed to give your son information and core skills.
