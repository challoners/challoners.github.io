About

Expand Your Knowledge

Homework Heroes allows you to answer questions based on your ability in a range of subjects from A-Level and GCSE. Also people can answer your questions and help you learn.

Learn Together

When you ask and answer questions you help everyone, them, you and anyone who read your questions or your answers meaning to Homework Heroes lets everyone learn together.

Customise Your Character

Homework Heroes is different to anything else that you will find, by answering questions, you can upgrade and customise your own character who can help you in quizzes and give you learning tips.
